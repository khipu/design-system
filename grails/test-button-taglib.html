Use Cases - Ejemplos Reales

1. Formulario de Pago

<kds:button variant="outlined" color="secondary" onclick="history.back()">Cancelar</kds:button>
<kds:button type="submit" variant="contained" color="primary">PAGAR AHORA</kds:button>

2. Confirmación de Mandato

<kds:button type="submit" variant="contained" color="primary" loading="${processing}">
Confirmar Mandato
</kds:button>

3. Acciones de Card

<kds:button variant="text" color="info" size="small">Ver Más</kds:button>
<kds:button variant="text" color="error" size="small">Eliminar</kds:button>

Integración en Grails

Uso en GSP

<g:form action="save" method="POST"> <!-- Campos del formulario --> <div class="kds-flex kds-gap-3 kds-mt-5"> <kds:button variant="outlined" color="secondary" onclick="history.back()" style="flex: 1;"> Cancelar </kds:button> <kds:button type="submit" variant="contained" color="primary" loading="${processing}" style="flex: 1;"> Guardar </kds:button> </div> </g:form>
